Make one extra monthly payment to accelerate a loan
Your base mortgage is $100,000 at 11.4% over 30 years. How much sooner will it be paid off if you make an extra payment, equal in amount to your regular monthly payment, every December? To access the Advanced Amortization screen, press Alt+x.
Enter the loan information at top: date, rate, amount, and 360 periods. Press C to compute the monthly payment. Press <H> over that number to harden the value as input. Then copy the amount from the line immediately above, and specify one payment per year in December. Finally, erase the Number of Periods box and press C once more to see how quickly the loan is paid off. (The number of monthly payments appears in the top row, the corresponding number of yearly payments below.)
