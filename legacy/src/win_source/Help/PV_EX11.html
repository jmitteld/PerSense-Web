Example 11: Pay cash or buy on time
You can pay $3500 for a new copier, or you can pay $88/month for four years. Which should you choose?
This is a common question, faced by purchasing agents and consumers every day. A useful way to think about it is to compare the cost of borrowed money to the rate you would lose if you withdrew money from the bank, or to the rate you would have to pay if you borrowed the money elsewhere.

Set up the Present Value screen with the purchase price and today's date in the bottom block. Leave the rate to be computed.

Enter the payment schedule above in the Periodic Payments block. Press C to compute the rate.
