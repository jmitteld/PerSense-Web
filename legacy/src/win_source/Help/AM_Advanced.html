The Advanced Amortization Screen
When the Amortization Screen is active choose "Advanced Options" under the "Calculate" menu and the screen will launch the remainder of PerSense's powerful loan amortizer, with options for:
  • multiple balloon payments
  • variable interest rate
  • specified payment amount over any fixed time period
  • delayed principal repayment (principal moratorium, or interest-onlypayments)
  • up to three different series of periodic payments
  • skipped payments
  • periodic skipped payments
  • targeted principal reduction (fixed payment to principal)
