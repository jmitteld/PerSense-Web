Computing a rate of return on investment
A share of stock is bought March 5 for 94.5. Dividend 2.25 is paid July 1. The stock is sold Sept 12 for 102.5.
To compute rate of return, leave Rates blank and put the original investment in the Value column. Enter the dividend and sale price as Single Payments. (For bonds or stocks held for a longer time, the Periodic Payments block may be useful for entering dividends.)
