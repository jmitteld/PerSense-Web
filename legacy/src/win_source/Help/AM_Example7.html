Change from a monthly to bi-weekly payments to accelerate a loan
Your base mortgage is $100,000 over 30 years. How much sooner will it be paid off if you take half the monthly payment and pay that amount every two weeks (Approximately equivalent to 13 monthly payments.)
Enter the loan information at top: date, rate, amount, and 360 periods. Press C to compute the monthly payment. Then halve that amount and re-enter it ($491.34). Chance 12 times per yr to 26, and erase the 360 periods. The number of periods will be re-computed when you press C again. (Loan is repaid in 506 2-week periods, or about 19.5 years.)

Note: 365-day calendar should be invoked for bi-weekly peymants. The appropriate settings will be changed automatically.
