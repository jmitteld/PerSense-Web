Flexible entry
Like all Per%Sense screens, the Amortization screen can work with any combination of data that you enter, as long as it logically determines the outcome. In the above example, we entered the Loan Rate and Number of Periods, and let Per%Sense compute the monthly Payment. You can also enter the monthly Payment and Number of Periods, and Per%Sense will compute the Rate. If you enter both the monthly Payment and the Loan Rate, Per%Sense will fill in the Number of Periods. You can even leave the amount of the loan to be computed, by filling in the Payment amount and Number of Periods.

If the amortization of complex, structured loans is important in your business, you'll want to read more about the Extended Options in the chapter on the Amortization Screen. For now, let's move to a different kind of computation on the Present Value screen. Press Alt+P or choose Present Value Screen from the Screens menu to bring up the Present Value Screen.
