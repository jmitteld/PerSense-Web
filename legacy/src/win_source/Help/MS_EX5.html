Example 5: Low points or low base rate?
Which is better, an 8.1% mortgage that carries 3 points or an 8.5% mortgage with 1 point?
Often in comparing mortgage possibilities at different banks you will be faced with the choice of paying less up front for points or paying less on a monthly basis.

You should make this choice based on how long you think you will be holding the mortgage: If you will be selling the house soon, then you won't have very long to enjoy the low monthly payment, so you should probably stick with the lower points. If you will be living in the house for a long time, the points are a one-time charge and may not matter so much in the long run.

Where do you draw the line? PerSense will do this computation for you, comparing two mortgages that differ in this way.

First enter the basic data for each mortgage. (The Price, the % Down, and the Monthly tax are all irrelevant to this comparison, and you may fill them in at random.)
