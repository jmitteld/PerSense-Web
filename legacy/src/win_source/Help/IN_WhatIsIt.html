What is Per%Sense?
Per%Sense is a tool for economic analysis, its three data sheets are so flexible and general in their format that almost any financial question you can imagine fits logically and neatly into one of them.

Per%Sense is a completely flexible calculator. Your job is to place the numbers you know in appropriate rows and columns of one of Per%Sense's data sheets. Per%Sense knows when you have provided sufficient information to solve the problem, and fills in the answers as you press Enter.

Your input numbers are hard data, displayed in a non-bold font on the screen; Per%Sense's output is soft data, displayed in a bold font. Soft data is erased and recomputed each time you make a change.

Behind the scenes, Per%Sense's intelligence is at work, analyzing the combination of data you have entered, determining what quantities can be computed, selecting the appropriate algorithms or formulas, executing them, and routing the answers to the appropriate cells on your screen. You have only to read the output.
