A window prompts you first for the column(s) you wish to vary. Column numbers appear temporarily at the head of each column for easy identification. In this case, you want to vary the loan rate (column 7). Press 7 , then Enter . The next line of the window prompts you for the increments between lines of the table. If you want lines to see results at % intervals, type .25 Enter . Next, how many lines do you want to generate. To go from 7% to 9%, you need 8 intervals of % each.
