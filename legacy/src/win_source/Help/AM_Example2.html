Remaining loan balance as of a given date
A $330,000 loan is paid in monthly installments over ten years, begining June 1987. If all payments have been made on schedule up through January, 1992, how much would be required to pay off the remaining balance on February 1?
Enter the amount, rate, and dates of the loan. Enter the date 2/92 in the date cell in the lower right. Press C to compute, and read the required amount from the adjacent cell labeled "Balance as of." Alternatively, you could print a table Ctrl+T and read the "Outstanding principal" column for the date in question. In this case, remember to add the amount of the regular payment that was assumed to have been made before the "Outstanding principal" was computed.
