Given price and cash available, compute monthly payment
Your client has $80,000 from the sale of another house to use as a downpayment. What will be his monthly payment on a 30-year mortgage taken on a $300,000 house?
Specify Price=$300,000 and points= (for example) 1.5. Skip the % Down column and enter 80,000.00 in the Cash Required column. Fill in Years, Rate, and Monthly Tax+Ins. Then press C, or choose Calculate, to read your answer from the Monthly Total Column.
