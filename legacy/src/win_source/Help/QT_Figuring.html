Figuring the payment
You can compute the monthly payment if you know the purchase price, the target purchase price if you know the monthly payment, or the balloon amount if you want to specify both price and monthly payment.

Enter the amount you will pay for your house in the first column. (The illustration uses $100,000.)

Press Enter or Tab to move to the next column, labeled Points. (Points are a one-time bank charge at the time a loan is taken out. 2.5 points means 2.5% of the amount borrowed is paid to the bank at settlement or closing.) Enter the number of points charged on your mortgage.

Enter the down payment percentage in the column labeled % Down.

Tab over to the Yrs column and enter the duration of your mortgage.

Enter the interest rate in the Loan rate column, and any additional monthly payments to the bank in the Monthly Tax and Ins column. (If you make no additional payments, leave the Tax and Ins column blank.)

Press Enter or C and the monthly payment amount will appear in its column.
