Example 3: Terminating a loan with a balloon payment
You're arranging a construction loan for $300,000 at 8.5 percent interest. $4,000 per month is to be paid for eight months while construction is taking place, and the remaining principal will be due the ninth month. How much is the final payment?
Enter the information as given, plus a Loan Date, a 1st Payment Date, and 12 Payments Per Year. Note that you are specifying the payment amount, rather than leaving it to be computed. Press Ctrl-T to create a table. PerSense takes your information and builds the table, then automatically tacks on a final payment that makes the numbers work out.
