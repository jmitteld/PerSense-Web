Car purchase: choose low percentage dealer financing or cash rebate?
Should you accept 4-year, 1.3% financing from the car dealer, or withdraw money from a 5.4% money market to pay $13,500 price less $1000 cash rebate?
Your plan for this computation is to compute the car payments at 1.3%, then calculate the REAL rate you're paying for financing by using a purchase price of $12,500.
Enter the payment schedule in Periodic Payments, leaving amount blank.
Enter the high price in the first Present Value line at bottom, with 1.3% loan rate and today's date. In the line underneath, enter the true price.
