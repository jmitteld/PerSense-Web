What Does Per%Sense do?
Every economic decision that we make involves the trade-off of economic costs and benefits. Sometimes there are intangible elements involved - uncertain outcomes and unknown probabilities. But often a great part of the analysis involves analyzing cash streams at different times. One of the things Per%Sense does best is to reduce complex cash flows at different times to common terms so that they can be computed directly.

Some applications in this category include:
  • Analyzing annuities and other payment structures.
  • Valuing legal claims for damages.
  • Financial planning.
  • Investment analysis and internal rate of return (IRR).
  • Comparison of business options - which is the best deal?
  • Deciding whether to lease or to purchase.
Another major focus of Per%Sense involves loans. Many programs will calculate a payment for regular monthly installment loans. Per%Sense will, in addition, allow you to structure a loan to meet the parties' particular needs:
  • Multiple balloon payments.
  • Interest-only for a period of time.
  • Fixed payment to principal.
  • Skipped payments.
  • Combinations of monthly, weekly, and quarterly payments for the same loan.
