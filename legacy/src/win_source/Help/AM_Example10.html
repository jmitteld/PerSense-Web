30-year amortization terminated at 12 years wiith a balloon
A loan for $260,000 at 9.1% is to be paid off on a 30-year schedule until the end of the 12th year, at which time it is to be terminated with a balloon payment.
Enter the loan information at top: date, rate, amount, and 360 periods. Press C to compute a monthly payment. Then move the cursor to the payment cell and harden the amount by pressing H. This fixes the payment amount so it won't be re-computed. Change the 360 to 144 payments. With Advanced Options turned on, re-compute the loan with C. Notice that a balloon payment has automatically been added for you on the last regular payment date.
