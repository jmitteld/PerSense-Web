The Mortgage Screen
The Mortgage screen is designed for choosing among different loan options.
  • if you want to know how much house you can afford on your budget...
  • if you want to find the loan with the lowest APR...
  • if you need to decide between a larger downpayment and a larger monthly payment...
  • if you want to evaluate whether it is worthwhile to pay more points to obtain a lower base interest rate...
  • if you want to evaluate the effect of a balloon payment on your monthly payments...
  • if you want to compare multiple loan scenarios...
then this is the best place to do it.

To get to the Mortgage Screen you have a number of options:
  • Select the "Screens" menu and choose "Show Mortgage"
  • Right click on the background to bring up the context menu and select "Show Mortgage"
  • Press Alt+M
