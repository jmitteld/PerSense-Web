Composite interest cost of a bond issue with multiple bonds
A company floats three bond series simultaneously: $20 million with 9% coupon, maturing in 7 years; $30 million with 9-1/8% coupon maturing in 10 years; and $30 million with 9-1/4% coupon maturing in 15 years. If all are sold at par, what is the cost (composite interest rate) of borrowing $80 million?
