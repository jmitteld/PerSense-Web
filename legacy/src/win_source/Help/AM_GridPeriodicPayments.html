Periodic Payments Grid
In addition to the regular periodic payments listed on the first line, PerSense allows two more sets of periodic payments directly below. For example, on a basic monthly loan schedule, you may add an extra payment to principal every quarter; or specify different payment amounts in even and odd months. This same line can be used for periodic skipped payments: For example, you can enter 1 Times per year and 0 for the Payment amount to skip every December payment.

The fields are:
  • First Pmt Date - The date of your first periodic payment.
  • # of Pds - Number of periodic payments.
  • Pmts /Year - Number of payments you will be making per year.
  • Last Pmt Date - The date of the last periodic payment.
  • Payment - Amount of each payment.
