Fixed payment to principal with a terminal balloon
A $50,000 loan is to be repaid with payments equal to interest + $1000 toward principal each month. At the end of two years, the loan is to be terminated with a balloon payment.
Set up the loan amount and dates, but enter 0 for the payment amount. Then specify Targeted Principal Reduction = $1000. The T.P.R is interpreted as a minimum, and will govern all payments since the regular amount is zero. Read the amount of the terminal balloon in the "Balloons" block.
