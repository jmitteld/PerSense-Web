Reverse mortgage
In a reverse mortgage, nothing is borrowed up-front, but the borrower receives monthly payments from the bank, generating a loan that grows over time.
To model a reverse mortgage, simply set the loan amount = 0 and fill in the monthly payment from the bank to the borrower as a negative value.

Press Ctrl+T to view a table showing the balance of the loan at any point in time, divided into interest and principal parts.

To compute the payoff amount on a known date, you can, as always, use the "Balance As Of" area in the lower right of the screen.
