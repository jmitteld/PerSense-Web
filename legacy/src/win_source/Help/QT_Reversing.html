Reversing the calculation
Now test Per%Sense's ability to "fill in the blank."

First select the whole line and press Ctrl+C to copy the line already entered.

Move the cursor down to the second line and press Ctrl+V to paste the contents of the first line into the second. Next Tab over to the column labeled Monthly total. Press H to "harden" the figure.

This turns the calculated number into an input number (as if you had entered it), so that the next time you press Enter , Per%Sense will use that number in its computation rather than trying to calculate it from other numbers.

Press Shift-Tab until you return to the Price column, and erase the price with the Del key.

Press Enter and the same computation you just did will be repeated in reverse. The result should be within a few cents of the price you entered originally. (Your monthly payment has been rounded to the nearest penny, and a difference of half a penny in the payment amount can translate into a difference of several cents in the price.)
