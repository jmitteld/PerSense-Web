Skipped months (payments in some months and not others)
A seasonal resort business requires a loan in which payments are to be made in December, January, and June-September. No payments are due in the other 6 months. Calculate a payment amount which makes this loan work out over 10 years.
Enter the loan information at top: date, rate, amount, and 120 periods for the full 10 years. Move the cursor to the borrom of the screen, to the cell for Skipped months. List the months in which there are to be no payments. When you re-compute the loan (Press C)a payment amount will be calculated which takes this schedule into account.
