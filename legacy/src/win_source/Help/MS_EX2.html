Example2: How much house can you afford?
You have $56,000 from the sale of your condominium, and you're able to make payments of $1650 a month, including taxes and insurance of $200 a month. The bank is charging 1.5 points and 8.5 percent interest on a 30 year mortgage.
This is the first practical illustration of the PerSense fill-in-the-blank interface: A screen is like an equation where you can solve for X or Y or Z just by leaving that cell blank and filling in the others. In this case, we want to reverse the usual procedure and compute the price of the house given the monthly payment. Simply fill in the data you know, and as you press Enter , the unknown price is filled in for you.
