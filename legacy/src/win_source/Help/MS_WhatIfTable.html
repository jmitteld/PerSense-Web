"What if" Tables...
Sometimes it's helpful to see the results of several different financing scenarios before deciding on a purchase.
  • What if the down payment is increased?
  • How does the monthly payment depend on the loan rate
  • What is the impact of different balloon amounts?
The Mortgage screen is equipped to create a table of scenarios starting with any line in the screen. The information in one or more of the cells can be varied by an increment you specify, and PerSense automatically generates a new line for each set of numbers.
