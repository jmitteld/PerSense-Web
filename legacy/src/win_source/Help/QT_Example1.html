Example 1: Winning the lottery
You've just one a $1,000,000 jackpot in the state lottery. The jackpot is paid in monthly installments of $4,000 over 20 years, with a bonus of $40,000 at the end of that time. What is the real value of the jackpot, in present dollars?

First, enter the monthly payments in the upper-right hand block. The first payment is next month, and the last payment is 20 years from this month.

Next, put the $40,000 in the upper left, along with the date of that payment, 20 years hence.

Now, press PgDn twice to move to the lower block of the screen. Enter today's date, you can press T and a fair market interest rate. Press Enter one more time, and the total present value appears at the lower right.
