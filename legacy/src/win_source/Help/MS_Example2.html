What price can you afford, given cash now and a monthly budget?
Your client has $80,000 from the sale of another house to use as a downpayment. He can afford $2000 per month for mortgage payments, which must include $600 for tax and insurance. How much can he afford to pay for a house?
Leave the Price column empty and enter Points=1.5. Skip the % Down column and enter 80,000.00 in the Cash Required column. Fill in the Years, Rate, and Monthly Tax+Ins. Enter 2000 in the monthly Total column. Then press C, or choose Calculate, and read your answer from the Price column.
