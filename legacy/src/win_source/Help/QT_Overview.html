Learning Per%Sense: Quick Tour
This chapter will quickly acquaint you with Per%Sense's major features. You will get the most out of the tutorial if you follow it with Per%Sense running and experiment freely as you go.

Per%Sense is a set of three independent calculation screens:
  • Mortgage screen
  • Amortization screen
  • Present Value screen
Many straightforward problems can be solved on more than one screen, although typically one screen is the most natural environment for setting up a rticular problem.

In general, you solve a problem in Per%Sense by recording your data in the appropriate places in one of the three pre-programmed data sheets. Press letter C or calculate, and the answer appears (in bold type) in its appropriate cell.
