The Present Value Screen
The Present Value screen is a very flexible tool for working with the time value of money. Applications include financial planning, structured legal ettlements, valuation of legal claims, annuities, pensions, and internal rates of return (IRR).

As in all PerSense screens, this flexibility is realized simply by choosing which cells you fill in and which you leave for the program to fill in. For example, you compute a present value by listing payments at the top, filling in date and rate at the bottom, and look for your answer in the Value cell at the lower right. To compute an IRR, you do the same, except fill in the Value column and leave the Rates blank, to be computed.
To get to the Present Value Screen you have a number of options:
  • Select the "Screens" menu and choose "Show Present Value"
  • Right click on the background to bring up the context menu and select "Show Present Value"
  • Press Alt+P
