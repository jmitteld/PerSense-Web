Specify monthly payment for the first 5 years
You wish to borrow $125,000 on 1 January 1990, paying 11.5% over 10 years. During the first 5 years you want to pay only $1500 per month, and make up the difference with a larger monthly payment thereafter. What is the amount of the payment during the second 5 years?To access the Advanced Amortization screen, press Alt+x.
Enter the amount and date of the loan. Fill in both the rate=11.5 and the payment=1500. In the box labeled "Rate Changes," enter the last payment date at 1500, and next to it 11.5%. Persense will use the payment you supply up through the specified date, and compute a correct payment thereafter so that repayment is completed on schedule.
