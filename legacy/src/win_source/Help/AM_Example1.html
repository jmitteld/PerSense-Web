Calculate basic loan payment and generate an amortization table
On January 14, 1992, your loan for $100,000 goes to settlement. On that date you prepay the first half month's interest, and regular payments are to begin March 1, continuing for ten years. Print out a quarterly summary of interest and principal payments.
Enter the amount and date of the loan, and 120 months. Check that the setting for Prepaid Interest is in force. Press C to compute payment amount, or Ctrl+T to generate an amortization table.
