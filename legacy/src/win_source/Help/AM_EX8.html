Example 8: Balloon unknown
A $75,000 loan is received in March 1995. Interest is computed at 10 percent and payments are to be $800 per month over 10 years, with a single balloon payment in March, 2000 taking up the slack. How much will that balloon payment be?
Enter the basic loan information on the first line.

With Extended Options on, press PgDn to move to the Balloon Payments block.

Enter the balloon date without an amount.

Press C to calculate the balloon payment.
