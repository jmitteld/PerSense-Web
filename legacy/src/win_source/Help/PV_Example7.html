Is it advantageous to pay cash or buy on time?
A new copier costs $3500 if you pay cash, or you can pay in 48 monthly installments of $95. Which should you choose?
A convenient way to think about this is to ask what your true interest % will be, and to compare that to the interest you would earn on the same money if it stayed in the bank. Enter dates of the first and last payments along with their amount in the upper right block. Enter today's date and the alternative cash amount in the bottom block. Press C and read the interest rate. This number can be compared directly to the rate your money is earning in a savings account (or to your cost of borrowed money).
