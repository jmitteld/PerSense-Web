Skip one monthly payment every year
A loan for $75,000 at 8.4% is to be paid off in monthly installments over a 10 year time frame. Payments are to be the first of each month, except there are to be no September payments - only 11 payments per year. To access the Advanced Amortization screen, press Alt+x.
Enter the loan information at top: date, rate, amount and 120 periods. Turn on the Advanced Options, and enter an additional series of payments just below the first set: one payment per year, begining 9/1. with amount=0. Be sure that the relevant Computational settings Alt+S is set to Stated balloon includes regular pmt: YES." This means that the specified balloon=0 is intended to INCLUDE the regular payment, i.e., that the total payment each September 1 is zero.
