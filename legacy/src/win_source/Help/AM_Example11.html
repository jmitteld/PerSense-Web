Fixed payment to principal
A $10,000 loan is to be repaid with payments equal to interest + $300 toward principal each month, continuing until the loan is fully repaid.
Set up the loan amount and dates. Enter 300 in the cell labeled Targeted Principal Reduction in the middle of the bottom of the screen, and enter 0 in the payment cell at the top. This will insure that the principal reduction target takes over in determining each payment amount. Each payment will then consist of exactly 300 in principal, plus a variable amount for interes. Press C to determing the loan duration. As you would expect, the loan is paid off in 34 payments (10,000 / 300).
