Example 1: Computing Monthly Payments
You purchase a house for $200,000 and take a 20-year mortgage at 8 percent with two points. You put down 20 percent and expect monthly taxes and costs to be $200. What is the total amount due monthly?
Enter the price, points, and down payment percentage. PerSense computes the cash required and the amount of your loan. Enter the number of years, the loan rate, and the extra monthly costs. As you press Enter for the last time, the monthly payment appears in the column labeled Monthly total.
