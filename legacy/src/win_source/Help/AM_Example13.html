Monthly interest plus yearly principal payments
For this loan, regular payments of interest only are scheduled monthly, and principal payments are made in addition once a year.
Set up the loan amount and dates. Be sure that the computational settings Alt+S for "Stated balloon includes regular payment" is set to "NO." Then enter the monthly payment schedule in the top line, leaving the amount blank. Specify in the bottom block "Principal payment delayed" the last payment date - this tells the top line to be interest only. The second line should include the annual principal schedule, with the annual principal amount spcified explicitly.
