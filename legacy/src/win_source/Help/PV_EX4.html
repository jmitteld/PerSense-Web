Example 4: How long will your nest egg last?
In Example 3 above, you decide to pass up the annuity and deposit your $150,000 nest egg in an account that earns 6.5% interest. You plan to withdraw $1000 each month for living expenses. How long will it be before you empty the account?
This variation can be done by filling in both the rate and the value at the bottom of the screen, but leaving the Through date for PerSense to compute.

Note: Although you entered $1000 as the monthly withdrawal, PerSense changed the number slightly to make the payments continue for a whole number of months.
