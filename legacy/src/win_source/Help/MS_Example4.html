Terminate a 30 year mortgage with a 15 year balloon payment
A 13% mortgage is taken out for a $275,000 house, with a 20% downpayment. Taxes and insurance cost $234 per month. The monthly payments are to be computed for a 30-year loan, but a repayment will be completed at the end of the 15th year with a balloon payment.
Fill in price=275,000, 20% down, 30 years, 13%, and 234 for tax and insurance. Press C to compute the monthly payment. Move the cursor over the Monthly Total call and harden it with H. Change the number of years to 15, and also enter 15 in the column labeled Balloon Yrs. Press C once again to compute the amount of the final balloon.
