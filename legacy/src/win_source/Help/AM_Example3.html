Adjustable rate loan
On 1/1/85, a 20-year adjustable rate loan was issued for $250,000 at 14%. In 6/86, the rate was lowered to 13.5%, and the payments were adjusted accordingly. Then in September, 1988, the rate was again lowerd to 12.5%. Print an amortization schedule, assuming the rate stays the same for the remaining life of the loan. To access the Advanced Amortization screen, press Alt+x.
