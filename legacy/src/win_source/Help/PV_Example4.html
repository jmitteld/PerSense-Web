Yield of a discounted loan: original interest rate known
How much should you pay for a discounted loan in order to realize a 12.6% rate of return? The loan was originally taken in August, 1986 for 200,000 at 14%, and runs through August, 1996. It is now June, 1994.
First compute the monthly payment by entering the original loan data as shown here and pressing C. Move the cursor to the Amount cell in the Periodic Payments block on the upper right, and press letter H to harden the calculated value. Then change the From date above to the next payment date (7/1/94). Press C again, and read the Value from the second Present Value line below.
