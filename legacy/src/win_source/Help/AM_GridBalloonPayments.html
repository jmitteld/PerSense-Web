Balloon Payments Grid
PerSense has room for up to 127 lump sum payments at any time during the life of the loan. Balloon payments must not be listed before the first regular payment. Any one balloon may have an unknown amount, and the amount will be filled in so as to make the loan repayment complete.

A balloon payment of 0 can be used to skip a single payment; (you must confirm that the Computational Settings screen shows Stated balloon includes regular payment: YES.
