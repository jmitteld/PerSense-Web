COLAs
Annuities, pensions, and structured settlements often provide for increases tied to the cost of living. In order to "keep up with inflation," payments are a few percent higher each year than they were the year before. Use the column labeled COLA for present value computations involving rising (or falling) payments.

The COLA column is optional. PerSense computes all other columns on the Present Value screen if you leave them blank, but not this one. A blank COLA column is the same as COLA = 0 percent.

Note: It is customary to quote inflation rates and COLA values as percentages that are not intended to be compounded during the course of one year. uch a number is technically not a rate but a yield. Following this convention, PerSense interprets numbers entered in the COLA column as yields, not rates.
