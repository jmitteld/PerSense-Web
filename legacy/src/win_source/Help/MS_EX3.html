Example 3: Balloon payment amounts
You want to buy a house offered at $280,000. Monthly tax and insurance costs come to $300. With a 20 percent down payment, the bank will provide 30-year financing at 8.25 percent with 2.5 points. You wish to keep your monthly payments below $1600 by scheduling an 8-year balloon. How much does the balloon need to be?
Note: One common variation on the straight monthly mortgage is a balloon payment. The monthly payment is reduced in exchange for the promise of a lump sum payment at an agreed future date. The Mortgage screen will include a balloon payment in the computation of monthly payments, or calculate a balloo payment that completes a mortgage with known monthly payments.
