Skipped Months Grid
Occasionally a loan will be written with regular monthly payments in just some months of the year, and other months skipped. For example, a teachers' loan may have regular payments except in July and August; a golf course may negotiate a loan agreement in which payments are made only in May-October. Per%Sense allows you conveniently to compute such loans, using the block of the screen labeled Skip months. Simply list all months in which there is no payment in this space. For example "2,3,6-8" will be interpreted to mean there are no payments in February, March, June, July or August. When you compute a monthly payment amount, Per%Sense knows that these months are skipped, and raises the remaining monthly payment accordingly. Balloon payments and extra payments are unaffected by skipped months.
