How big a balloon is needed to complete a mortgage?
You wish to purchase a house for $180,000, paying 20% down, and taking a 30-year mortgage at 10.5%. Including $250 each month for tax and insurance, you can afford $1400 payments. You want to make up the difference with a balloon payment at the end of ten years. How big must the balloon be?
Specify Price=$180,000, Points=1.5, 20% Down, 30 years, and 10.5 "Rate." Fill in the Monthly Tax+Ins, and put the 1400 in Monthly Total. In the space for Balloon Yrs, fill in 10 years, but leave the amount blank, to be computed. Then press C.
